Cape Town Snapshot

Everything you need to know for a successful conference experience

Safety

Cape Town is generally safe for conference delegates. Our partners provide secure transfers and vetted accommodations.

Currency

South African Rand (ZAR). USD widely accepted. Credit cards accepted at major venues and hotels.

Weather

May is autumn with mild temperatures (15-22°C). Pack layers for conference rooms and outdoor activities.

Dress Code

Business formal for conference sessions. Smart casual for networking events and tours.
